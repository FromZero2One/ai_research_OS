# NVIDIA Corporation Investment Analysis

## Business Overview
NVIDIA designs and manufactures graphics processing units (GPUs) for gaming, professional visualization, data centers, and artificial intelligence. The company has transformed from a gaming GPU manufacturer into a dominant force in AI computing.

## Key Products
- **Hopper H100**: Data center GPU for AI training and inference
- **Blackwell B200**: Next-generation AI GPU platform
- **CUDA**: Parallel computing platform and programming model
- **DGX Systems**: Integrated AI supercomputers

## Financial Highlights
- Data center revenue grew 5x since FY2023
- Gross margins exceed 70%
- Strong free cash flow generation
- Zero net debt balance sheet

## Competitive Position
- Estimated 80%+ market share in AI training chips
- CUDA ecosystem creates high switching costs
- Competition: AMD MI300X, custom ASICs from hyperscalers
- Export controls to China may limit addressable market

## Risks
1. Demand normalization after AI infrastructure buildout
2. Increasing competition from AMD and custom chips
3. Geopolitical risks from US-China trade tensions
4. Valuation at elevated multiples
